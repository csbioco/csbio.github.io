DNA Synthesizer

High Load Oligo Synthesis

As an instrumentation company that has designed and manufactured custom peptide synthesizers for over 25 years, CSBio has custom adapted synthesizers for use in the DNA/RNA oligonucleotide synthesis industry for pilot and process scale manufacturing. Utilizing our CDMO and instrumentation experience, CSBio has developed a DNA synthesizer and has been synthesizing oligonucleotides over the past year with outstanding results.

Pilot Scale

The CS-OLIGO-D is a fully automated DNA/RNA synthesizer that can be used for oligonucleotide synthesis at the pilot and commercial scale with capacity of 0.2 mmol to 7mmol synthesis.

This system includes a high load resin reaction vessel with our patented 180º inversion mixing with flow through, while also allowing for traditional synthesis with recirculation.
