Introduction

At CSBio, we regularly work with labs and universities that have never made a peptide before. In the process of providing them with a peptide synthesizer, we give them hands on training to quickly allow them to start making peptides.

In this mini-course, we provide an overview on what peptides are and how to synthesize peptides.
