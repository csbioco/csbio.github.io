30+ Years | 30+ Countries

CSBio has been designing and manufacturing fully automated peptide synthesis instruments for over 30 years, and has systems installed and utilized in more than 30 countries worldwide. Starting with the first automated peptide synthesizer on MS-DOS, CSBio has honed the systems to the flexible, reliable, and cost effective equipment they are today.

CSBio builds peptide synthesizers from research scale to commercial scale manufacturing, with many of our systems being custom built to order. CSBio has produced more automated pilot scale and commercial scale peptide synthesizers than any manufacturer in the world.
